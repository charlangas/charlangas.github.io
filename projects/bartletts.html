For the past 150 years, Bartlett's Book of Familiar Quotations has been amassing over 20,000 carefully curated and categorized quotes, printing them in edition after edition of the book.

But for their latest edition, publisher Hachette decided to do something different and kickstart a new era for the book with an iOS and Android app.

One of the main challenges was designing a clean interface which would highlight the beauty of individual quotes, but which would still allow users to easily find each of the app's 20,000 quotes.
