I was born and raised in Mexico, but as a designer I grew up in New York City. After attending SVA as an advertising major, I fell in love with the possibilities of the web and was lucky enough to join the talented folks at POKE New York as a designer. A couple of years later I returned to my hometown of Guadalajara to launch a startup. I learned a lot, but my co-founders and I decided the sanest thing to do was to close the company. Failure makes you strong. Now I'm available for freelance work as a web designer and a data-driven digital strategist.

Please feel free to email me if you'd like to chat :)
